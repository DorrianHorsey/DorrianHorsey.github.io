"I have no special talent. I am only passionately curious."

the summary

Everyone has a story. Mine began with a great family just outside Cleveland Ohio. After a few new characters, changes of scenery, and plot twists (aka life experiences), it has come full circle to a similar setting just a few miles from where I grew up. The end.

the backstory

Oh, you were expecting more? I told you I'm an introvert, but here it is. I've always had an insatiable desire to learn that is the core of everything I do personally and professionally. I started my career in sales and training. Sales taught me how to communicate with anybody and that true professionalism means empathy for the client. The desire for greater challenges led me to law school and to a legal career that spanned two states. Practicing law gave me the ability to break down complex concepts for diverse audiences and allowed me to incorporate research and writing and project management skills into my professional toolkit. As my career evolved, so did my family and I took some time away from professional life to establish that foundation. During that time, I built a few simple websites using Html and css and started looking into adding a technical skill set to enhance my other professional skills.

the here and now

I am engaged in a 5-star-rated coding boot camp, WeCanCodeIT. learning full stack development from expert instructors and intensive hands-on projects. Software development fundamentally relies on my favorite aspect of my previous opportunities: meeting the needs of clients through innovative problem solving. Using technology to accomplish that goal is both challenging and thrilling. I am eager to take everything I've learned to this point and apply it to a technology role as my career continues to evolve in the future.
